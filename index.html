ABOUT HACK IN THE NORTH


A Hackathon? What's that?

A Hackathon is a collaboration of programmers and designers to code, design and build innovative solutions to real life problems, through extensive coding in a short time duration. Hackathons are the biggest thing to happen to education since the rise of the Internet. See this for a good explanation.

Your only constraint is: You only have 30 hours.

India's Premier Hackathon

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch - these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots. So prepare yourself for a weekend filled with learning new technologies, building amazing products and making new friends all within the span of 30 hours.

Everyone is Welcome

Whether you're making your first website, hacking Oculus Rift to censor real world ads, or creating something fun; InOut is for you. We'll have great workshops, mentors, and more! We will allow you to show your coding skills without getting worried about any mortal needs. We will take care of everything.

FAQ


What is HackInTheNorth?

HackInTheNorth is the annual Hackathon run at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad. It is one of the largest student held hackathons in India


How is HackInThenorth run?

HackInTheNorth is run by Aproksha team and GeekHaven(Technical Society of IIIT Allahabad)


Can we apply as a team?

Not quite — you and your intended teammates should all submit individual applications. But if you get an invitation and some of your teammates don’t, shoot us an email at geekhaven@iiita.ac.in and we’ll try to sort things out.


What should I bring?

We’ll send you a comprehensive list once it’s closer to showtime, but it’ll be basically what you’d need for any sleepover: laptop, charger, deodorant (!), change of clothes. You won’t need food or drinks; we’ve got you covered there.


How and when will I hear back?

We’ll be releasing decisions weekly — if you’ve submitted your application by a Friday, you’ll be getting an email back by the next Friday!.







Who can apply?

If you’re enrolled in a high school, college, or university, or if you left school during the current school year, you’re good to go!.


What do people normally make?

You can make anything you like. Most of the hacks revolve around web or phone apps, or working with hardware.


What are you looking for in an application?

Above anything else—passion. Tell us what you care about, what excites you. We want to know what’ll drive you to start and continue hacking, whether it’s your first time or your fifteenth. If there’s something about you that stands out from the “typical” hacker, we’d love to hear about that, too!.


How much does it cost to attend?

Zero. HackInTheNorth is free for everyone.


Will you be offering travel reimbursements?

We would support you if you can't afford to travel to HackInTheNorth. Shoot us a mail if you need reimbursements.


When are the application closing?

Applications close March 20! If you haven’t submitted by then, we’ll review whatever’s there..
